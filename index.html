About Us

Our family has been raising this amazing breed for over 20 years now. We searched high and low for the perfect breed that was not only hypoallergenic, but also a great companion dog. The Coton de Tulear is the royal dog of Madagascar and they are great, low impact dogs. Perfect for first time dog owners, busy families, or someone looking for a lap dog, these guys have it all! This breed is very versatile, often referred to as the "velcro dog" or the "antidepressant dog". Our puppies are happy, healthy, and ready to take on any adventure with you.
